# Superstore Sales Analysis - Query Objectives

## Explore Data

The queries in this section were used to understand the structure of the dataset, preview sample records, and verify the available columns before starting the analysis.

---

## Validate Dataset

This section was used to validate the quality and completeness of the dataset by checking:

- total number of rows
- unique customers
- missing values in important financial columns

---

## WHERE Filters

The queries in this section were used to retrieve filtered business information such as:

- sales performance in the West region
- high-value technology products
- highly discounted products and their profitability

---

## GROUP BY Aggregations

This section focused on summarizing data using aggregation functions to analyze:

- regional sales and profit
- category-wise performance
- customer segment contribution

---

## Sorting & Limiting

The queries in this section were used to identify:

- top-selling products
- highest revenue customers
- products generating the highest losses

---

## Business Use Cases

This section was used to solve real business problems and extract meaningful insights such as:

- yearly business growth
- monthly sales trends
- identification of loss-making sub-categories
- duplicate order validation
